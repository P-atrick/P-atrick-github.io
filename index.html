About Me

Having discovered the world of fintech startups during my Economics degree I decided on a change of direction into tech, a logical choice as I've always been heavily into computing, having built my own computers and played with basic web development many times. I started by studying Information and Communication Technology in Stockholm for a year where I discovered a preference for the creative side of tech.


During my research for where I could go next I found the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly, a full time intensive 12 week course. I loved the course and now know enough about full stack web development to become a Junior Web Developer. Learning to code has been immensely enjoyable and I still get to use some of the skills from my Economics degree, making sure to take a methodical step by step approach to my work and only beginning once a plan of what I want to do has been thoroughly thought out.

[ IN PROGRESS ] - Review Compiler

This project is to build a website that takes review scores for cars from multiple websites and produces an average score. Individual cars are displayed both in a table showing all of the scores and beneath the table in cards. If a user goes to a car's card they can view more information about it such as which gearboxes and fuels it is available with.

Made with: HTML5, CSS3, jQuery
